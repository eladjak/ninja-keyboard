# Ninja Keyboard - Merged Character Bible v2.0

**Purpose:** Definitive reference for every character in the Ninja Keyboard universe.
Merges the original v9 spec (Ki, Mika-original, Bug, Sensei Code, Glitch, Pixela) with the new Gemini-generated cast (Ki-v2, Yuki, Luna, Noa, Kai, Mika-tech, Sensei Zen, Pixel, Rex, Shadow/Storm/Blaze).

**User decisions applied:**
- Tal (non-binary) DROPPED
- Sensei Code MERGED INTO Sensei Zen (turtle design kept)
- Original Mika (accuracy partner) ABSORBED into current Mika (tech hacker) with expanded role
- Bug and Glitch ADDED as new characters requiring Gemini art generation
- Shadow/Storm/Blaze stay as battle rivals

## Part 3: 3-Act Narrative (Merged)

### Act 1: "The Awakening" (Lessons 1-7)

**Story:** Ki discovers a magic keyboard in an old dojo. The keyboard glows when typed on correctly. Mika, who was already training in secret, appears and challenges Ki. They become partners. Bug appears for the first time and starts scrambling letters.

| Lesson | Story Beat | Characters Present |
|--------|------------|--------------------|
| 1 | Ki discovers the magic keyboard. Sensei Zen's voice echoes: "Welcome, young ninja..." | Ki, Sensei Zen (voice only) |
| 2 | Ki practices home row. Ki makes mistakes, laughs at himself. | Ki |
| 3 | Mika appears: "You're too slow." They challenge each other. Partnership begins. | Ki, Mika |
| 4-5 | Training montage. Learning upper/lower rows. | Ki, Mika |
| 6 | Bug appears! First scrambles a few letters. "Hehe, mess!" | Ki, Mika, Bug (intro) |
| **7 (Mini-Boss)** | **"Bug the Small": Bug scrambles 10 words. Player fixes them in 60 seconds. Bug escapes.** | Ki, Mika vs Bug |

### Act 2: "The Training" (Lessons 8-15)

**Story:** Sensei Zen physically appears (not just voice). He reveals the dojo's history and begins training the team properly. New allies join: Yuki (speed), Luna (focus), Kai (battle), Noa (healing). Bug grows stronger, scrambling entire sentences. Glitch appears as Bug's chaotic ally.

| Lesson | Story Beat | Characters Present |
|--------|------------|--------------------|
| 8 | Sensei Zen appears in person. "To defeat Bug, you must master every key." | Sensei Zen, Ki, Mika |
| 9 | Full keyboard training. Shift, numbers, symbols. | Ki, Mika, Sensei Zen |
| 10 | Yuki challenges the team to a speed race. "I'm the fastest!" | Yuki (intro), Ki, Mika |
| 11 | Luna appears in the Moonlit Garden. "Slow down. Breathe. Then type." | Luna (intro) |
| 12 | Glitch appears! Flips the keyboard layout. "G-g-glitch is h-here!" | Glitch (intro), Ki, Mika |
| 13 | Kai joins: "I'll protect you while you type." Noa heals scrambled text. | Kai (intro), Noa (intro) |
| 14 | Bug scrambles entire paragraphs. Team works together. | All heroes |
| **15 (Mid-Boss)** | **"Bug + Glitch": 20 words + fix error insertions. Glitch flips some keys. Bug escapes again.** | All heroes vs Bug + Glitch |

### Act 3: "The Ninja" (Lessons 16-20)

**Story:** Bug builds the "Error Maze" -- a digital labyrinth of scrambled text. The team must navigate it. Each hero contributes their specialty. Glitch briefly helps (he is confused about whose side he is on). Final Boss: Bug King -- Bug at full power, wearing a tiny crown.

| Lesson | Story Beat | Characters Present |
|--------|------------|--------------------|
| 16 | Enter the Error Maze. Bug: "Welcome to MY world!" | Ki, Mika, Bug |
| 17 | Mika hacks through firewalls (keyboard shortcuts). Luna finds the calm path. | Mika, Luna, Pixel |
| 18 | Glitch wavers. Player types accurately to "calm" Glitch. Glitch briefly helps. | Glitch (redemption arc), Ki |
| 19 | Kai holds off Bug's minions while the team types through. Noa heals errors. | Kai, Noa, Yuki |
| **20 (Final Boss)** | **"Bug King": 50 words in 2 minutes, 90%+ accuracy. Bug wears a crown. ALL heroes help. Combo system: Ki=speed, Mika=accuracy, Yuki=burst, Luna=focus, Kai=power, Noa=healing. Bug escapes ("I'll return!"). Team celebrates.** | ALL characters |

**Post-Game:** Certificate of "True Keyboard Ninja." Bug escapes, setting up future content. Credits roll with chibi character art.
